Are all courses compulsory?

No, you can opt for any course of your choice.

Are all courses inter-related?

No, but you will see a growth in your technical knowledge if you opt for all three.

Do I need to bring my own laptop?

Yes, you need to bring your own laptop. We will provide you with an internet connection.

Is the course useful for campus placement and final year projects?

Yes, the course can help you solve technical questions of various IT companies and the knowledge gained will be an aid for your final year project.

What will I get at the end of the course?

At the end of the course you will begin building your Github profile, make projects, and have a certificate validating the course.

What are the batch timings and how will the batches be allotted?

We have two batches: 9am to 11am and 6pm to 8pm. Batches will be allotted on first come first serve basis.

Who can opt for the course?

The course is focused for engineering students, although anybody (even with no prior coding experience) can apply.
